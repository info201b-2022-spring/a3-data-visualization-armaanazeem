Bar graph depicting Alaska's population ethnicity proportions in respect to the total population (ages 15-64). The proportions are calculated from the total average population size throughout the 48-year span.

Bar graph depicting Alaska's jail population ethnicity proportions in respect to the total jail population. The proportions are calculated from the total average jail population size throughout the 48-year span.
